### **ğŸ¯ Example: Create a Perfect Square Box**


```xml

```

### **ğŸ“Œ Explanation:**

* `width: 80vw;`

* `aspect-ratio: 1/1;`  
    This ensures that the height always equals the width, keeping it a perfect square.
